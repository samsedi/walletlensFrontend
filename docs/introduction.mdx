## Overview

WalletLens provides an institutional-grade risk scoring and on-chain forensics API for Ethereum wallets. Our tools help you clear the fog of decentralized finance by providing instant clarity on risk vectors.

### Endpoints at a Glance

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Wallet Details" icon="wallet">
    Fetches a 0-100 risk score, historical interaction metrics, and specific risk flags (like OFAC sanctions or mixer exposure).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Transactions" icon="chart-line">
    Retrieves historical transaction counts grouped by days, weeks, or months to easily track wallet activity trends over time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ENS Resolution" icon="at">
    Provides forward and reverse ENS resolution alongside rich social metadata like Twitter handles and avatars.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Chat" icon="robot">
    Powers an interactive chatbot capable of reasoning about a wallet's risk profile in natural language.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
